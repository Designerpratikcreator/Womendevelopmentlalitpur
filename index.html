About

Lalitpur Women Development is an organization based in Lalitpur, Nepal. It promotes various programs and initiatives focused on womens development. We advocate for the rights of women in Lalitpur and work to ensure their fundamental human rights. We offer schemes and programs aimed at empowering women in all aspects of life.

Women Education

  • Photography – Become a skilled photographer.
  • Research and Development – Explore and learn through focused study.
  • Vocational Skills – Learn practical skills and earn from them.
  • Awareness Programs – Participate in public speeches and awareness events.

Our Programs

Skill Development Workshops

Providing hands-on training in various vocational skills such as tailoring, computer literacy, and handicraft making to foster economic independence.

Health and Wellness Camps

Organizing regular health check-ups, awareness sessions on reproductive health, hygiene, and nutrition for women in the community.

Legal Aid and Counseling

Offering free legal assistance and counseling services to women facing issues related to domestic violence, property rights, and gender discrimination.

Entrepreneurship Support

Supporting aspiring women entrepreneurs with mentorship, micro-finance access, and business development training to help them start and grow their ventures.
